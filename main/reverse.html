Reverse Engineering Services and Research

Reverse Engineering is an art where one has to not just reverse engineer the product at hand, but also recreate the thought process of the original developer to be able to accurately deduce every implementation detail in the product.

The team at Selective Intellect™ is a collection of such accomplished artists who excel at reverse engineering software products built for any system. We have recently completed two DARPA Cyber Fast Track contracts and are on the way to completing our third one by the end of this year in the area of reverse engineering and embedded hardware security.

Our expertise in internal details of operating systems along with knowledge of various software compilers together with our in-house reverse engineering tools enables us to reverse engineer software with high accuracy and satisfactory results. The knowledge base we have includes Intel and AMD processor based systems that run Microsoft Windows® or Linux®, and the ARM systems in the mobile world such as that of iOS® and Android® platforms. We also have expertise in reverse engineering firmware of microcontrollers and FPGAs based on behavioral analysis of the hardware and other techniques that are proprietary.

We perform a detailed tear-down of the product to be reverse engineered, and provide an extensive report on how the product has been created along with its various functionalities. The results of our analysis are supplied in a custom format tailored to our client's needs.

We provide the ability to modify the product's internals themselves to work as per our client's requirements, without having complete access to source code.

Our team is open to any challenges thrown our way and we believe our in-house expertise and tools enables us to have a distinct advantage in reversing software systems.
