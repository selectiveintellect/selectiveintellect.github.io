About Us

Selective Intellect™ was founded in 2008 with an aim to provide cutting-edge solutions focused on our team's specialized skills in the fields of firmware development, high performance computing and compiler development. We have many years of experience and have successfully completed numerous contracts for our clients in the aforementioned fields.
Over the years the company has broadened its focus and is moving towards tackling problems in operating system internals, compilers and developer tools, and FPGA design and development.

We also have been a Department of Defense sub-contractor and continue to do so in the fields of firmware development, high performance computing and operating system integrations.

Our vision is to provide innovative solutions to exceedingly complex technological problems that are deemed too difficult to solve and to successfully advance these fields.

Our commitment to the open source community is visible in our products Wisecracker™ and Hotpatch™ being open source themselves.
